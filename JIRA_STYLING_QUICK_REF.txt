--------------------------------
- Color Contrast: Minimum 4.5:1 for normal text
- Alt Text: Required for all visuals (meaningful descriptions)
- Keyboard Navigation: Logical tab order

- Semantic Colors: Never rely on color alone (use icons/text)

5. Use 20px gaps between visuals
